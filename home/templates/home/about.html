About Fotolio

Original cinematic photography prints, ready for your space.

Fotolio is a small, focused collection of original photography designed to live on real walls – not just on screens.

Every image is captured, edited, and curated by the same creator, so the gallery feels consistent in mood, contrast, and storytelling. You won’t scroll through thousands of random styles – just a tight selection that works together.

The idea is simple: make it easy to choose prints that feel cinematic, modern, and considered – whether you are building a full gallery wall or just looking for one strong hero piece.

What we focus on

Original photography only – no stock, no AI.

Small, curated selection instead of endless scrolling.

Cinematic mood with rich contrast and controlled light.

Print sizes chosen to work well as wall sets.
